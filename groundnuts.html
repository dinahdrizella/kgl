Groundnuts

Groundnuts are a nutrient-rich snack, providing:
1. Protein: Groundnuts are an excellent source of plant-based protein.
2. Healthy Fats: Groundnuts are rich in healthy monounsaturated and polyunsaturated fats.
3. Fiber: Groundnuts are a good source of dietary fiber.
4. Vitamins and Minerals: Groundnuts are a good source of vitamins E and B3, as well as minerals like potassium and magnesium.

Culinary Uses Groundnuts are incredibly versatile and can be used in a wide range of dishes, including:
1. Snack Foods: Roasted groundnuts, peanut butter, and peanut brittle are just a few examples of popular snack foods made from groundnuts.
2. Savory Dishes: Groundnuts can be used in stir-fries, curries, and stews for added protein and flavor.
3. Baked Goods: Groundnuts can be used in baked goods, like peanut butter cookies and peanut brittle.
